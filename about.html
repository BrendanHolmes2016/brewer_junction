WELCOME TO BREWER JUNCTION!

A Little About Brewer

Welcome to Brewer Junction, home of the best "Brews" known to man. Every year we hold an annual "Brew" tasting welcome to all surrounding communities who like to drink and have a good time. This is followed by a parade and firworks. Come visit the beautiful Brewer Junction.
